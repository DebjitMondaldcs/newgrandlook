Services

That we provide.

Haircut

Short, layered bob with a side-swept fringe. The back is tapered and the ends are textured for movement and volume. Finished with a smooth blowout.

Keratin Treatment

Keratin treatment is a hair smoothing process that uses protein to straighten and eliminate frizz. It can last up to six months and requires low maintenance.

Straghtning Smoothning

Straightening smoothing is a hair treatment that uses a combination of heat and chemicals to relax curly or frizzy hair, leaving it straight and smooth with reduced volume and frizz.
